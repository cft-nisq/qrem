simulation.py: Quantum State Simulation under CTMP Model

Part of the CTMP subpackage, this module simulates the evolution of quantum states under the influence of quantum errors as modeled by the CTMP model. It uses Gillespie‚Äôs algorithm to model state transitions over time.
